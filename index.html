Contact.ie: Service Unavailable

After 11 years of providing the easiest way for people to contact politician in Ireland, including running some very popular and successful email campaigns, I have decided to stop the service.

Over the past 2 or so years, I have not been able to put enough time into the service, and it has not been as popular as it was in previous years. I have a feeling people prefer to just post messages on Facebook or Twitter to politicians rather than taking the time to send an email, or write a letter, or call in person. I hope I'm wrong about that.

The source code for Contact.ie is still available. If anyone would like to host a copy of the website, feel free to clone the repo and do so.

Thanks for all your support over the years. You can continue to follow my adventures on Twitter or my blog - mark.ie.

Original Contact.ie source code is freely available on GitHub.
